No More Plastic Bags

Litters in the ocean

Plastic bags account for a large amount of plastic litters in the ocean.

Plastic bag usage every minute

2,000,000 bags

According to Global Citizens, 2 million plastic bags are used every minute around the globe.

Difficulty of degradation

500 years

It takes more than 500 years to break down a plastic bag in the soil.

Why does it matter?

Damage to sea animals

Plastic bags in the oceans cause sea animals to suffocate to death and harm their health if they are consumed by mistake.

Damage to environment

Most plastic bags are unrecyclable, so they are landfilled or burn which cause more problems such as, contributing to global warming by burning them.

What can we do as individuals?

Avoid using plastic bags & Share the issue on social media

  • Reusable grocery bags are reusable and durable.
  • One post can spread the issue out to many people.
